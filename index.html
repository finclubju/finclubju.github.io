FAQs

What is the main focus of FinClub's work?

FinClub is the largest finance and consulting oriented club in Jadavpur University and its activities focus on finance, financial awareness, investment, trading, consulting, business and related fields.

What kind of events are conducted by FinClub?

FinClub's long agenda for the year includes seminars, workshops, mock trading and case study competitions, quiz and panel discussion sessions, awareness programmes and many other interactive events.

When was FinClub founded?

FinClub is a student-run club and was founded in 2021 by a group of finance enthusiasts. Since then the club has grown to become the first and the largest finance and consulting oriented club in the university.
