Greetings from Finance Club, Jadavpur University!

We are a team of finance and business enthusiasts on a mission to promote financial awareness. We help young individuals aspire higher, develop their dreams and make it their destiny, by nurturing a strong finance culture and instilling business acumen among the student community.

Read our Story - our History, Mission and Vision

FinClub's journey started on a random October afternoon in 2021 with a small group of dedicated students who would not stop before making their dream a striving reality, and since then there has been no looking back. Read the story of our ups and downs, and learn more about our mission and our vision.

    We provide a wide array of services

    From conducting training and career development sessions for students to providing pro-bono consulting services and financial software solutions to clients, we get things done.

    Training and Upskilling

    Preparing students for careers in finance and consulting remains our first priority. Read more.

    Financial Software Solutions

    Our solutions include FinLabs, algo backtesting, data feeds, mock trading and financial calculators. Read more.

    Consultancy Services

    Our offering includes low-cost and pro-bono consulting for corporate and social impact firms. Read more.

    Partnerships and Collabs

    We partner with financial education providers and collab with college clubs and societies. Read more.

    Our 6 Pillar Work Structure

    FinClub's diversified work structure includes a board of advisors with faculty and alumni, an executive team, 6 operational teams - treasury, editorial, PR & marketing, logistics, tech and design, and 6 functional teams based on our 6 pillars of work - finance, investment, social impact, consulting, analytics and literacy.

    Finance

    Financial services career, industry insights and more.

    Investment

    Student-run portfolios, stock market training and more.

    Social Impact

    Pro-bono consulting services for corporate & social firms.

    Consulting

    Consulting career, case studies and more.

    Analytics

    Analytics reports, tools, infographics and more.

    Literacy

    Awareness programs, literacy drives and more.
