About FinClub - Our History, Mission and Vision

FinClub's journey started on a random October afternoon in 2021 with a small group of dedicated students who would not stop before making their dream a striving reality, and since then there has been no looking back. This is the story of our ups and downs, our history, mission and vision - the story of the largest community of finance, investment, consulting & analytics enthusiasts in JU.

Our History

Jadavpur University is a premier public research university in Kolkata, and an Institute of Eminence recognized by the University Grants Commission. Ranked 4th among Indian universities by the National Institutional Ranking Framework, Jadavpur University is home to some of the brightest minds in West Bengal and India at large. With a growing interest in finance, consulting and analytics among students and increasing placements in these domains, there is a demand for more workshops, seminars and learning events as well as the right guidance for participating in finance and case competitions and for taking up live projects for startups, NGOs and business clients. Therefore, we saw the creation of a Finance Club in JU as the need of the hour. The purpose of establishing the Finance Club was to provide the perfect place and the best available resources for students to explore the worlds of finance and consulting, and enhance their knowledge, and to promote, encourage and facilitate discussion on and understanding of everything business - business news, financial markets, the financial services industry, business consulting and analytics, as well as exploring and preparing for career opportunities in finance and consulting.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a strong business culture which benefits the entire student community and provide a platform for upskilling and professional growth and development, a shared investment in knowledge which is destined to yield huge returns for all. We have distinguished the Finance Club from other clubs and societies by adopting a structured and organized approach towards learning and career development through seminars, lectures and workshops on finance and consulting topics, emphasizing on networking with alumni and other professionals, providing a route map for proper practice and experience through participation in national and international case solving and finance competitions, and finally by encouraging application of acquired skills by doing client projects. Our ultimate mission is to impart knowledge in a comprehensive and organized form so that every member joining our community with a desire to explore and learn, leaves as a confident individual with a strong understanding of the financial world.

Our Vision

Our vision behind establishing the Finance Club included but was not limited to providing quintessential financial knowledge in an organized form, supplemented by appropriate training for global contests, in an effort to provide the right exposure to the community of finance and consulting enthusiasts in JU. Our vision resonates with that of finance clubs and societies in colleges across India – to foster an ecosystem of growth and learning among our members, prepare them for careers in finance and consulting and provide them with a reliable and exhaustive reference in these domains. We are motivated by our goal of helping our members build their network and connect with the smartest minds in their field. We believe that participation in intra and inter college events and competitions, combined with interaction with like-minded individuals, will provide our members with avenues for further personal and professional growth, in line with our vision and aim to empower and educate future finance and business leaders.

Club Membership, Team Recruitment and Board of Advisors

We're a unique community, the first of its kind in Jadavpur University. And, we are always looking forward to more enthusiastic students and alumni to join our community. If you are a student currently enrolled in any undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma or doctoral program under any faculty in Jadavpur University and you wish to explore finance, investment, consulting and analytics, you can join FinClub by filling the membership form at finclubju.com/join. If you are interested in applying for any role within the FinClub team, you can do so by applying online at finclubju.com/apply. If you are a Jadavpur University alumnus currently working in finance, investment, consulting or analytics and you wish to be associated with FinClub in an advisory and mentorship role, or if you wish to conduct a training or interaction session for students, drop an email at finclubju@gmail.com.
